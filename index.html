Phooli

The storyline revolves around the young girl Phooli, who wants to study and pursue her dreams, it depicts her struggles and her eagerness to study but her circumstances do not allow her to continue with her education, later a Jaadugar (Magician) enters her life and helps her overcome her situations and motivates her to excel in her studies through magic.
The film Phooli is an initiative to encourage the motto of Sarv Shiksha Abhiyan and Beti Bachao Beti Padhao therefore we want this film to reach every corner of our nation be it a child or an adult. This film is an emotion which is quite relatable to the underprivileged children of our society who are willing to reach for the sky yet are unable to find the right direction.
